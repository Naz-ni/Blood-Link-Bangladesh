The Challenge: A Fragmented System

Bangladesh's current blood ecosystem faces critical gaps in coordination, logistics, and technology, leading to preventable delays and loss of life. We've identified the key bottlenecks that our platform aims to solve.

Fragmented Donor Systems

Multiple unverified apps, university clubs, and social media pages exist with no central database. This causes families to waste precious hours searching for donors.

Transportation Bottlenecks

With Dhaka's average traffic speed at a mere 6.4 km/h, traditional transport like ambulances are too slow. There is no dedicated, optimized fleet for urgent blood delivery.

Hospital & Data Gaps

Hospitals lack real-time inventory systems and a national dashboard for blood availability. This, combined with a lack of AI-driven demand forecasting, creates critical inefficiencies.

National Blood Supply Sources

A heavy reliance on replacement donors from family and friends introduces risks and delays. A key goal is to significantly increase the share of safe, voluntary donations.

An Integrated, End-to-End Solution

Our platform connects every stakeholder in the blood donation journey through a seamless, technology-driven process. The workflow below details how we ensure rapid and reliable delivery.

1.

Platform Request 🏥

When a patient needs blood that is not in stock, a trained hospital coordinator initiates an urgent request through the national platform dashboard. The request includes blood type, component needed (RBC, platelets, etc.), and urgency level.

2.

AI Donor Match 🎯

The platform's AI instantly queries the verified donor registry, filtering by blood type, location, and availability. It identifies and alerts the most suitable donors via app notifications, SMS, and automated calls to ensure a rapid response.

3.

Optimized Transport 🏍️

Simultaneously, the system's AI routing engine selects the fastest transport option based on real-time traffic data. It dynamically dispatches the best choice from Tier 1 (Uber/Pathao), Tier 2 (Vetted Volunteers), or Tier 3 (In-house emergency fleet).

4.

Collection & Cold Chain 🩸

Blood is collected either at the donor's home or a nearby partner facility. It is immediately placed in a portable, temperature-monitored cold-chain box (2-6°C for RBCs) to ensure viability and safety throughout its journey.

5.

Hospital Delivery 📦

The transport unit delivers the blood directly to the hospital's transfusion coordinator. A secure digital handoff is completed using QR codes, and the inventory is updated in real-time on the platform, closing the loop.

Unifying the Ecosystem: Our Partner Integration Model

We are not starting from scratch. Our core strategy is to connect the fragmented, existing community platforms and databases into a single, verified national registry.

🏫

University Networks (e.g., Badhan)

Tap into existing student donor pools and run targeted campus campaigns.

How We Integrate:

  • API Integration: We will provide secure APIs to university blood clubs for real-time data sharing and registration.
  • Formalizing Process: We will help them formalize donor screening and data collection, feeding verified donor profiles directly into our national registry.
💬

SMS & Alert Systems (e.g., Rokto)

Utilize their proven SMS infrastructure for rapid, wide-reaching emergency alerts.

How We Integrate:

  • Alert Orchestration: Our platform will be the central hub, sending AI-matched requests to their SMS systems, which will broadcast messages to their existing donor base.
  • Data Partnership: We will merge their donor databases into our central registry, enhancing the scale and reliability of our network.
👥

Community Pages (e.g., BloodLine)

Merge donor databases to enhance social engagement and community building.

How We Integrate:

  • Formalization of Networks: We will provide these ad-hoc groups with a structured digital presence, enabling them to verify and manage their members more effectively.
  • Centralized Registry: Their valuable, local donor data will be migrated into our single, national registry, making it accessible for wider, more urgent needs across the country.
🏍️

Ride-Sharing (e.g., Uber/Pathao)

A multi-modal transport strategy to overcome Dhaka's severe traffic bottlenecks.

How We Integrate:

  • Medical Express API: We will integrate with their APIs to launch a dedicated "Medical Express" service, giving our deliveries priority.
  • CSR Partnership: This offers them valuable public relations, and in return, we get access to a massive, nimble fleet of motorbikes that can navigate traffic far more efficiently.
💖

Volunteer Network

A vetted network of community drivers and coordinators.

How We Integrate:

  • Tiered Logistics: Our AI will use volunteers as a cost-effective, local delivery option when ride-sharing is unavailable.
  • Incentives & Training: We will provide small stipends for fuel, certification, and training to maintain a committed, reliable fleet of community heroes.
